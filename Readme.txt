Silly Putt

How to Play
=======
- Left and right arrows to move the camera counter-clockwise and clockwise respectively
- Hold the power button to increase the strength of a shot, and release to hit the ball
- Get the ball in the hole in as few shots as possible

Features
=======
- 3 x Levels
- 5 x Achievements
- Ads that play at the end of every second round (can hit level restart button twice to shortcut to ad playing)
- In-app purchase to disable ads
- Leaderboard at the end of each round to show the leading scorer
- Twitter integration
